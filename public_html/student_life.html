Strawberry University's Student Life

Strawberry University is ranked #2 in the world for it's student life by ThisIsAMadeUpWebsiteCalledStudentLife.org based on its diverse range of different activities available for all students to do 24/7. All dorms for students are former luxury apartments with more room than most know what to do with. All meals served at Strawberry University are prepared by the world's top chefs at no extra cost to the students. There are hundreds of clubs you can join and if none fit your needs, it is very easy to start your own, all you need is an idea and a couple people who would like to be apart of it.
